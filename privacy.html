Timgo — Privacy Policy

Effective 10 July 2026

Timgo is a segmented timer. This policy is short because the app is simple: Timgo does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. None.

What stays on your device

Your timers, presets, and sound preference are saved in your browser's local storage, on your device only. They never leave it. Deleting the app, or clearing the site's data, erases them permanently. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services of any kind.

Permissions

Timgo may ask for notification permission, used solely to tell you a segment has finished while the app is in the background. Notifications are generated on your device and involve no server. You can decline or revoke this at any time in your system settings; the timer works fine without it.

Network

After the first load, Timgo runs entirely offline. The only network activity is your browser fetching the app's own files from its host when an update is published.

Children

Timgo collects no data from anyone, including children.
